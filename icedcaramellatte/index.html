Name of Recipe:

Iced Caramel Latte


Level of Difficulty:

1/5


Servings:

1 person per drink


Diet Flexibility?:

Vegan - adjustment of diary

If you’re a major money manager or a broke college student, you can enjoy a good iced latte without breaking your bank.

Get the Ingredients!

  • Ice
  • Caramel Sauce
  • Vanilla Flavored Milk
  • Unsweetened Dark Cold Brew
  • An 8oz cup

Step 1: Caramel bottom

Cover the inside of your cup in caramel. An easy way to do this is by rotating the cut as you pour caramel on the inner edge.

Step 2: Ice Cubes

Pour in your cubed ice to about 1/4 of the cup you are using.

Step 3: Cold Brew

Pour your cold brew up to where the ice ends in your cup.

Step 4: Pouring the Milk

For me, I like using vanilla soy milk in my latte, but be free to use any milk. Experiment until you are satisfied. Pour in your milk to fill the rest of the cup

Step 5: Add more Caramel and Stir!

Take a sip and see if you want to add more caramel. And Presto!

You're Done! Enjoy your latte!
